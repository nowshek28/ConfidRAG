Title: FAQ & Glossary — Vision Systems

FAQ
   Q1: What are the standard working hours?
       A1: 9:00–18:00 JST; core time is 10:00–16:00 JST.
   Q2: How long can PII be retained?
       A2: Up to 90 days unless an approved exception exists.
   Q3: Does the camera support hardware trigger?
       A3: Yes, SenseCam X supports TTL trigger with debouncing.
   Q4: Can I use the REST API for inference?
       A4: Yes, POST /infer with an asset_id and model name.

Japanese Quick Answers（日本語）
   Q: コアタイムはいつですか？
   A: 10:00〜16:00（JST）です。

Glossary
   • HDR: High Dynamic Range imaging.
   • Debouncing: Ignoring rapid, spurious trigger edges.
   • Entropy: Measure of unpredictability in a signal.
